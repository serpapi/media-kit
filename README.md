# SerpApi Media Kit

Logo images, contact information, and other media resources for SerpApi.

### Brand assets

The SerpApi logo, wordmark, and banner images are available in three styles. The primary logo in full-color should be used in most situtations. If more contrast is required, use the light style on dark backgrounds, and the dark style on light backgrounds.

Please do not use any other colors for the logo or wordmark.

**[Download all files](https://github.com/serpapi/media-kit/archive/refs/heads/master.zip)**

### Press contact

For media inquiries, please send an email to press@serpapi.com. For technical support or other questions, please use the contact form or send an email to [contact@serpapi.com](mailto:contact@serpapi.com).

All legitimate SerpApi communications come from the serpapi.com or tryserpapi.com domains only.

### Brand name

The correct spelling is SerpApi for the company and all its services, with the S and A as the only capitalized letters.

Please do not use SERPAPI, SerpAPI, serpAPI, or other spellings.

### About SerpApi

SerpApi is the world’s leading provider of search data, pioneering APIs that turn search engine results into structured, developer-ready information. Founded in 2017, the company enables developers, researchers, Fortune 500 companies and organizations around the world to integrate live search insights from sources like Google, Bing, and YouTube into applications, analytics, and research workflows.

With more than 100 specialized APIs, SerpApi supports use cases ranging from AI development and machine learning to SEO monitoring, cybersecurity intelligence, and e-commerce analysis, helping teams build reliable, data-driven products at scale.
